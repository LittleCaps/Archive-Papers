## **Supernovae Driven Winds Impede Lyman Continuum Escape from Dwarf Galaxies in First 10 Myr**

 We find that LyC escape peaks early, during a period dominated by intense radiation and stellar winds but lacking a fast galactic wind. As the starbursts age, supernovae drive and accelerate the wind, progressively suppressing LyC escape. These results highlight the need for cosmological simulations to incorporate early feedback as a key driver of reionization.
